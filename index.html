About "ProjectPal"

What is ProjectPal?

ProjectPal is a webpage that can be used by students to create and organize tasks in a fun and visually appealing way.

How do I use ProjectPal?

Creating tasks is very easy!

  • - Type in the title of your task and then a description of what you need to do to accomplish that task.
  • - Set a Due Date for your task and give it a priority of "High", "Medium", or "Low".
  • - Click the submit button and it will generate a "Task Card" for you!

Are my tasks saved?

Yes! All tasks you make are automatically saved to your local storage on your computer. Every time you open up the page, it will regenerate all tasks you had previously.
